Red chilli garlic Ghee chutney

Wall textured and delicious flavor.

  • Ingredients for chutney

1/2 cup hot red chilli powder, dry.
1/3 cup chopped garlic.
1/4 cup curry leaves.
1 tablespoon fresh ginger, chopped.
2 teaspoons cumin seeds.
1 cup cow ghee (clarified butter).
salt to taste.

  • DIRECTIONS

Blend all the ingredients into a coarse paste. Do not add any water. Transfer to a bowl. Heat the ghee in heavy pan. Turn off the stove once the ghee starts the smoke. Add the chilli garlic paste to the ghee and stirr well. Be sure not to burn the chilli garlic chutney.
The ghee chutney is ready to serve. Enjoy with Prata or Puri!

  • Note

Place it in an airtight container and store in the refrigerator for instant use anytime. Heat before serving.
