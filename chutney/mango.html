Green Mango Chutney 'N Dip

Tangy green mango chutney.

  • Ingredients for chutney:

1/2 Medium size peeled green mango.
2 green chillies, chopped.
1 cup chopped fresh cilantro (Dhaniya).
1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin seed powder.
1/4 teaspoon roasted fenugreek seed powder (roasted Methi).
1/4 cup water.
salt to taste.

  • DIRECTIONS

Cut the mango into small pieces. Add mango pieces, green chilli, cumin powder, fenugreek powder, cilantro (Dhaniya), water and salt to a blender. Blend to a smooth paste. Serve as an accompaniment or condiment with snacks and Indian meals.
Store the green mango chutney in the refrigerator in an airtight container for instant use anytime.

  • chutney Dip Ingredients

1/2 cup green mango chutney.
1/4 cup water.
1/3 cup fresh curd (plain yogurt).
1/3 cup cream cheese.
1 tablespoon olive oil.
salt to taste.

  • DIRECTIONS

Add water, curd, cream cheese and salt to a blander and give it a quick pulse. Then add the green mango chutney and give it a few quick pulses to mix well. Pour olive oil over the dip. The dip is ready for nachos, pasta or as a spread on a sandwich or burger, or even as a salad dressing.
