Coconut Chutney 'N Dip

Coconut chutney has a rich and amazing flavor.

  • Ingredients for chutney:

1 cup sliced coconut.
1/2 cup chopped mint.
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro (Dhaniya).
1 tablespoon fresh ginger, chopped.
1 tablespoon garlic, chopped.
1 tablespoon green chili, chopped.
1/2 teaspoon rosted cumin seed powder.
1/4 teaspoon rosted fenugreek seed powder ( rosted Methi).
1/3 cup fresh curd (plain yogurt).
1/4 cup water.
salt to taste.

  • <

    For Tempering (Tadka)

2 tablespoons oil.
1 teaspoon mustard seeds.
1 teaspoon urad dal.
a pinch of hing / asafoetida
1 dried red chilli, broken.
a few curry leaves.

  • <

    DIRECTIONS

>Put the ingredients for chutney, incudling the water, in a blender and give it a few quick pulses. Transfer to a bowl. Start the tempering by heating the oil. Then add the mustard seeds, urad dal, cumin seeds, garlic, red chilli and curry leaves. Allow the tempering to sputter.
Finally, pour the tempering over the Coconut chutney and serve It!

  • <

    chutney Dip Ingredients

1/2 cup untempered Coconut chutney.
1/4 cup water.
1/3 cup fresh curd (plain yogurt).
1/3 cup sour cream.
1/3 cup cream cheese.
1 tablespoon olive oil.
1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic, fried.
salt to taste.

  • <

    DIRECTIONS

Add water, curd, sour cream and salt to the coconut chutney and give it a few quick pulses to mix well. Transfer to a bowl and stir in the garlic. Pour the olive oil over the dip. The dip is now ready for idlis, dosas, vada, nachos, pasta or as a spread on a sandwich or burger, or even as a salad dressing.

Rich in fatty acids, calories, vitamins and minerals
