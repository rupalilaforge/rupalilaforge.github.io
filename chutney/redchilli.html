Red Chilli Garlic Chutney 'N' Dip

Quick and delicious flavor.

  • Ingredients for chutney:

1/2 cup hot red chilli powder, dry.
1/3 cup chopped garlic.
1 cup water.
1/2 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro (Dhaniya).
salt to taste.

  • DIRECTIONS

Blend garlic, red chilli powder, water and salt into a coarse paste. Pour into a dish, add chopped fresh cilantro and stirr.
Serve with samosa, kachuri, pakora, Dosa, or any kind of fried food!

  • Chutney Dip Ingredients

1/2 cup red chilli garlic chutney
1/4 cup water.
1/4 cup fresh curd (plain yogurt).
1/4 cup shredded mixed cheddar and mozzarella cheese.
1/3 cup sour cream.
salt to taste.

  • DIRECTIONS

Add water, curd, shredded cheese, sour cream and salt to the red chilli garlic chutney. Give it several quick pulses in blender to mix well.
The hot dip is ready for Nachos, Pasta or to spread on sandwich, burger etc.
