Keeping Your Chutney Safe

Once made, a chutney will keep for a week or two, depending on how much salt and water you add. Keep it in a sealed container and it will freeze well.

Chutney lasts for long time

Chutny will keep indefinately in the freezer. But use small containers to avoid having to refreeze leftovers. One possiblity is to use an ice cube tray covered with an aluminum sheet. Don't forget to put a label on it. Chutney can also be stored in a dry, airtight container in the refrigerator.


Of course, power outages will greatly reduce the amount of time that your chutny can be kept. It is important that, once frozen, it is kept frozen until it is ready to be eaten.
