What to serve with the dips

The possibilities are endless.
Dips can be served with breadsticks, potato chips, taco chips, crackers, baby carrots, bell pepper strips And tortilla chips. And dips are perfect spread on toasted beagles. Or you can serve them with french fries.

Many Ways To Eat Chutney

Green chutney is a classic condiment in India where it is served with just about every meal. Chutney are made with mint, cilantro, green mango and other ingredients. These recipes are easy to alter. You can make them solely with cilantro, or you can increase the amount of chilli or mint. Do use some cilantro or the chutney will taste bitter. Serve it on the side of Indian dishes like idli, dosa, upma, poha etc. Have it on a sandwich. Or add it to any kind of salad.

Puree it and use as a dipping sauce for egg rolls, samosa, vada, patties, cutlets or any fried food! Serve it with a cheese or sliced meat platter.
Mix it into ground beef or pork and use for meatloaf or sausage roll filling.

Eat it with any kind of cooked pork dish.

Serve it with grilled sausages, chicken or kabab or roasted chicken or turkey.

Puree and serve with glazed meat, roasted sweet potatoes, corn, butternut squash, etc.

Puree it and add it to salad dressing.

Serve it with a grilled cheese sandwich.

Marinate chicken, turkey, pork, paneer or tofu with chutney.

Serve it on a cheeseburger, or on a veggie burger.
