Fire Roasted Tomato Chutney n' Dip

A particularly Yummy Chutney

  • Ingredients for chutney:

2 whole tomatoes.
1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro (Dhaniya).
1/2 teaspoon chopped green chilli.
salt to taste.

  • DIRECTIONS

Roast the whole tomatoes directly on the gas burner. Let them cool and then peel off the skin. Mash the tomatoes. Add salt, green chilli and chopped fresh cilantro. Enjoy!

  • chutney Dip Ingredients

1/2 cup fire roasted tomato chutney.
1/4 cup water.
1/3 cup fresh curd (plain yogurt).
1/3 cup cream cheese.
1/4 cup shredded mixed cheddar and mozzarella cheese.
Salt to taste.

  • DIRECTIONS

Put water, curd, cream cheese, shredded cheese, and salt in a blender and give it quick pulse. Then add the tomato chutney and give it few more quick pulses to mix well. The dip is ready for nachos, pasta or as a spread on a sandwich or burger, or even as a salad dressing.
