In a small  pan over medium-high heat, warm 1 Tbs. of the oil. Add the mushrooms, salt and pepper and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 4 minutes. Transfer the mushrooms to a paper towel-lined plate.

In the same pan over medium-high heat, warm the1 Tbs. oil. Add the onion,cloves,bell pepper, salt and pepper and cook, stirring frequently, until just softened.Add the basil Transfer the pepper to the plate with the mushrooms.

On a floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into a 12-inch round. Transfer to a floured pizza peel. Spread 1/2 cup tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border. Top with the cheese, then with the cooked vegees and in the top mixed cheese leyer.  
Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone. Bake until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is crisp, 12 to 15 minutes. Using the pizza peel, transfer the pizza to a cutting board and garnish with the freshly grated Parmesan cheese. Cut and enjoy!
