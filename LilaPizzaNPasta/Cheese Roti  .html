Cheese Roti

Ingredients

Oil for shelo friy.
Salt to taste.
1/3 cup of freshly grated Gouda, Mozzarella Cheese, Mixed.
2 Rotis

Method -

1. Tack a roti set in a plate and sprinkle the mixed cheese on full roti evenly. Sprinkle the salt and put another roti top of it.
2. Then in a frying pan heet up the oil on high and, when it is smokeing, add the roti. After 30 sec. turn and fry till golden brown. Cut with Pizza cuter. Now it is reedy to eat.
